About Empire Clicker

Empire Clicker is an addictive incremental game where you start with nothing but ambition and build your way to a thriving business empire.

Begin with simple clicks that generate small amounts of cash, then strategically invest in various business ventures. As your empire grows, unlock new opportunities, upgrades, and achievements.

With intuitive gameplay mechanics and satisfying progression, Empire Clicker offers both quick play sessions and long-term strategic planning.

How To Play

  • Click to earn your initial capital
  • Purchase businesses to generate passive income
  • Upgrade your businesses to increase profits
  • Hire managers to automate operations
  • Balance investments to maximize your empire's growth

Game Features

Multiple Businesses

Invest in and manage various business types, each with unique profit potential and upgrade paths.

Strategic Upgrades

Purchase upgrades to increase your clicking power, business efficiency, and income multipliers.

Manager System

Hire managers to automate your businesses and generate income even when you're not actively clicking.
